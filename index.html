About Ascalon
Ascalon is a company founded to fight loneliness and social isolation. We believe the world is a better place when everybody is surrounded by people they share a genuine connection with. We believe that health and happiness are unachievable without having fitting social fabric. Thats why we are creating Twine, the best tool to find the fitting people for life - and every occasion.

The company was founded in 2023 and is headquartered in Friedberg, Germany.
